Weekly Progression

  • Week 1: Learn form, use light/moderate weights
  • Week 2: Slightly increase weights (2.5–5 kg)
  • Week 3: Increase reps or weights
  • Week 4: Increase weights or reduce rest time to 60 sec

Extra Tips

  • Drink 2.5 to 3 liters of water daily
  • Sleep 7 to 9 hours per night
  • Optional: whey protein after gym
  • Cardio 1–2 times/week outside morning sessions
  • Focus on consistency, not perfection
